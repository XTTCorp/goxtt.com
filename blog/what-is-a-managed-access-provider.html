What is a Managed Access Provider?

Mar 13, 2022

What is a Managed Access Provider?

We get this question a lot, and its a good one! A Managed Access Provider (also known as an Internet Broker) like XTT is a business which bridges the gap between the local Internet Service Provider and the businesses and homes they provide service to.

What are the benefits of using a Managed Access Provider?

Everyone dreads making a call to their local ISP. You provide your account number and they ask you to do all sorts of complicated steps to bring yourself online and are always hesitant to send out a technician. Imagine a world where you called to report the outage and instead of asking what steps you've done they begin working towards diagnosing the problem. Then we work on troubleshooting and then push the ISP to send a technician to troubleshoot.

Do I really need it?

While you can operate without one, a Managed Access Provider gives you numerous benefits. Most internet providers do not support bonded circuits like we do, and many require you to hold a business account to guarantee uptime. On top of this, the ease of resolution when your home/business goes offline is incomparable. If you're still not convinced, reach out to sales@goxtt.com to learn more.
